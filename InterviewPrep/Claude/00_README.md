## How the rounds usually go (senior DevOps/SRE, tier-1 India)
1. **Recruiter screen** — motivation, comp, notice period, high-level fit
2. **Technical phone screen** — resume deep-dive + a few hands-on questions (sometimes light coding)
3. **Coding round** — DSA (some companies) or scripting/troubleshooting (SRE-flavored)
4. **System design** — design an infra/platform system, discuss trade-offs
5. **Behavioral / hiring manager** — STAR stories, leadership, conflict, ownership
6. **Bar-raiser / values round** (Amazon-style) — deep behavioral + culture fit

## Golden rules
- **Defend everything.** Never say anything you can't unpack for 5 minutes. All content here is from your real work.
- **No fake numbers.** Use concrete-but-honest proof points ("no manual credential handling anymore").
- **"I" vs "we"** — say "I" for what you did, "we" for team context.
- **Think out loud** in design/coding — they score your reasoning, not just the answer.
- **Ask clarifying questions first** in every design/coding problem.
